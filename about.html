About

Our Mission Statement

To establish an open community of aspiring designers, to inspire creative confidence, and to ease students into industry.

Core Values

We try out best to carry out our mission statement by instilling three core values into what we do:

Industry Ready

Help students feel confident that they can get into industry

Community

Get students involved with the design community.

Design

Emphasis on human-centered design.
